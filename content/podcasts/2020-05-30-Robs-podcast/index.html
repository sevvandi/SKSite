Are you interested in forecasting? Well, Anthony Mays and myself did a podcast with Prof Rob Hyndman from Monash University on this topic. This episode is a part of the ACEMS podcast series Random Sample and can be found at https://acems.org.au/podcast/episode-29-forecasting-the-future

In this episode, Rob explains time series forecasting really well. He gives such interesting anecdotes from his vast experience. I really enjoyed conducting this podcast, so much so that I forgot to take a photo. That is my only regret.
