Before the 2020 self-isolation period (also known as the lockdown) Tim Macuga from ACEMS and I informally interviewed Patricia Menéndez from Monash University and made a podcast. Even though it was an informal chat, it was pretty special because it was about Patricia’s Homeward Bound trip to Antarctica.

I felt she took us there with her evocative expressions. This podcast is part of the ACEMS podcast series Random Sample and can be found at https://acems.org.au/podcast/episode-24-Antarctic-Outreach where there are lovely pictures of Antarctica. Don’t miss it.
